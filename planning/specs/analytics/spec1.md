# Analytics Spec

## Overview
Define analytics and dashboard requirements for business metrics.

## Scope
- business views
- KPI calculations
- dashboard charts and tables
- Snowsight or UI reporting outputs

## Requirements
- total revenue, orders, customers, and average order value
- revenue by month, category, country, and payment method
- top products and top customers
- repeat customer and customer lifetime value metrics
- forecasted revenue and growth predictions
- forecast dashboard KPIs and charts

## Outputs
- `sql/06_views.sql`
- `forecasts/monthly_revenue.csv`
- `forecasts/revenue_forecast.csv`
- dashboard screenshots in `screenshots/`
